R-ECO Solitaire V1.2.1

Powerd by HTML5, without any Flash plugin.
Perform best in modern web browsers.

Game Rules:

Choose one color to dispose.

When the factory have 4 icons, take score.

After disposal, take all garbage from dump site into your hand.

Refull dump site to 1 more garbage then factory.

If more then 6 garbage in hand, you lose.

All scores of a color are taken, you win, and get additional 4 points.

* Click the score icon to immediacy take score, but only worth 1 point.

Normal mode has more scores, but only 5 garbage most in hand.


This game is well tested, best players can score 90+.

Have fun!
